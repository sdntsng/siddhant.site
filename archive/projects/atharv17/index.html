IPM, IIM Indore proudly bring to you the fourth instalment of its Cultural, Management and Literary Fest. Presenting,
Atharv'17 : An Animate Affair .

An Animate Affair

This year, at Atharv, come join us as we reminisce our childhood and bring to you the heroic, the villainous, the smartest and the funniest comic characters. Just come along with your masks and your capes and watch the magic unfold!

Management & Literary Events

Join the brightest young minds from all over the country as they battle it out in a clash of the intellect, creativity and talent. With events like IIM Indore IV, Imperium and Vendition Vendetta, we promise to make you think outside the box as you try to prove you're the smartest in the room.

Cultural Events

Be it Dance, Drama, Music or even just some Kickass Style, you bring the talent and we give you the stage. Bailar, Vanity and Clash of the Cults are just a few events to grab your fifteen minutes of fame and live your childhood dreams.

Pro Night

Come groove with us to the best music the country has to offer in the biggest party of the year. With Sunburn and VH1 Supersonic in our memoirs, it can only get better.

Comedy Night

We, at IIM-I, never fail to tickle your funny bones. From Biswa's "Mast Aadmi" to Zakir's "Sakth Launda" we've had it all. So just grab a bowl of Poha and join us in our laughter riot!
(PS: Be warned, there'll be plenty of Poha jokes!)

Informals

Unwind from all the intense competition with our Informal events. Wage war on your friends with Paintball, click wacky pictures at the Photobooths or make new friends at our informal nights, the possibilities are limitless!
